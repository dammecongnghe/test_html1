What Is Brainrot?

Brainrot is an internet term that describes a heavily meme-shaped way of speaking online. It shows up in school jokes, streaming communities, Discord chats, Reddit posts, and short-form video culture, where words get remixed into exaggerated slang that sounds half ironic and half completely serious.

Common brainrot references include skibidi toilet, Fanum tax, gigachad memes, sigma and alpha jokes, Italian brainrot like Ballerina Cappuccina and Tralalero Tralala, plus brainrot animals and characters such as Bombardino Crocodilo. A good brainrot translator helps make those phrases more readable without flattening the joke.

Brainrot Example

Brainrot: I'm not good at flirting, at best I'm average. I lock up. I've been messing up my chances lately. Maybe I should actually put in some effort for once.
Translation: I'm low on rizz, at best I'm beta. I get skibidi-locked. I've been yapping my rizz chances lately. Maybe I should actually goon and put in some sigma energy for once.
`Low on rizz` -> lacking charm or flirting skills
`Beta` -> average or not confident
`Skibidi-locked` -> frozen up or paralyzed by nerves
`Yapping` -> talking too much and messing it up
`Rizz chances` -> opportunities to flirt or connect
`Sigma energy` -> a more self-confident approach
