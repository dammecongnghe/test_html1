Understanding Indian Standard Time and Eastern Standard Time

The difference between Eastern Standard Time and Indian Standard Time varies depending on whether the United States is observing daylight saving time:

  • During EDT (March to November): India time is 9.5 hours ahead of Eastern Daylight Time
  • During EST (November to March): India time is 10.5 hours ahead of Eastern Standard Time

For example, when it's 3 PM IST to EST, the converted time would be 5:30 AM EDT (during daylight saving) or 4:30 AM EST (during standard time).

Important: India does not observe daylight saving time, so IST remains at UTC+5:30 year-round. However, the United States Eastern timezone switches between EST (UTC-5) and EDT (UTC-4), which is why the time difference changes twice a year.
