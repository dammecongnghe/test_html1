Roblox VC Voice Chat Guide PC & Mobile

How to Get
Voice Chat
on Roblox

⏱ 4 min read 🎙 Works on PC & Mobile ✅ Age 13+ required
Quick answer: To get voice chat on Roblox, you need to verify your age (13+) using a government ID, then enable the Voice Chat toggle in Privacy settings. The whole process takes about 5 minutes.
📋
Before you start — what you need

You must be 13 or older · A valid government-issued photo ID · A smartphone (for QR code scanning during age verification) · A Roblox account

Step 1 — Open Roblox Settings

This works the same on PC and mobile.

1
Open Roblox

Head to the Roblox website or open the Roblox app on your phone — either works for this guide.

2
Go to Settings

PC: Click the gear/cog icon in the top right corner.
Mobile: Tap the three dots (⋯) in the bottom right, then scroll down to find Settings in the menu.

3
Click Account Info

In the left sidebar, click Account Info. This is where the age verification option lives.

Step 2 — Verify Your Age

Roblox VC requires you to be 13+. You'll verify this with a real ID — it's a quick automated process.

💡 Safe to do: Roblox uses a regulated identity verification system. Your ID details are processed automatically and aren't stored by Roblox directly.
4
Click "Verify Age"

In Account Info, find and click the Verify Age button. An identity verification pop-up will appear.

5
Scan the QR code with your phone

Open your phone's camera app and point it at the QR code on screen. Tap the link that pops up. If your camera doesn't scan QR codes, grab a free QR scanner app from the App Store or Play Store.

6
Take a photo of your ID + selfie

Tap Let's Go, allow camera access, then take a clear photo of your government-issued photo ID. Then take a selfie. Make sure you're in good lighting — the process is fully automatic and only takes seconds.

7
Age verified ✅

Within a few seconds it'll say "Your age is now verified." Tap Close and head back to your computer (or stay on mobile — both work).

Step 3 — Enable Voice Chat in Roblox

Now that your age is verified, turning on Roblox VC takes one more step.

8
Go to Privacy settings

In Roblox Settings, click Privacy in the left sidebar. You may need to refresh the page first for your verified age to show up.

9
Toggle on "Enable Voice Chat"

At the top of the Privacy page under Beta Features, you'll see the Enable Voice Chat toggle. Switch it on, read and agree to the terms, then tap Enable.

✅ Done! Voice chat on Roblox is now active. You'll see a mic icon above your character's head when you join a game that supports it.

Mic Not Working? Fix It Here

If your mic isn't picking up in-game, here's the quick fix:

1
Open in-game settings

Press Escape or tap the Roblox icon in the top left to pause the game, then go to Settings.

2
Set the correct Input device

Under the audio section, change the Input Device to your actual microphone. Do the same for Output if needed, then unmute yourself.

⚠️ Note: Not every Roblox game supports voice chat. If you don't see the mic icon in a game, that game hasn't enabled it — try a different one.

FAQs

How do you get voice chat on Roblox?

Verify your age (13+) using a government ID through Account Info in Settings, then enable the Voice Chat toggle under Privacy → Beta Features.

Can you enable voice chat in Roblox on mobile?

Yes. The process is identical on mobile — tap the three dots, go to Settings → Account Info, and follow the same age verification steps.

What age do you need to be for Roblox VC?

You must be 13 or older. Roblox verifies this with a government-issued photo ID during the setup process.

Why can't I see the voice chat option in Privacy settings?

You likely haven't completed age verification yet, or the page needs a refresh after verifying. Complete the ID check in Account Info first, refresh the page, then check Privacy again.

Is Roblox voice chat available in all games?

No — only games where the developer has enabled it. If you don't see a mic icon when joining, that game doesn't support voice chat yet.

That's the full guide to getting voice chat on Roblox. Verify your age, flip the toggle in Privacy, and you're ready to talk. If your mic acts up in-game, just check your audio input device in the game settings and you're good to go.
